Day1-Data in My Life

Phone Screen Time
Who collects this data: digital well being settings in my mobile phone.
What decision it helps make: Helps me decide how to reduce distractions and manage my daily time better.

Sleep Duration 
Who collects this data: By me or my mom
What decision it helps make: Helps me to decide if I need to improve my sleep schedule for better health.

Exam Marks
Who collects this data: My college teachers
What decision it helps make: Helps me to understand my strengths and weaknesses and decide what subjects to focus on.

Daily Rainfall in My City
Who collects this data: Meteorological Department
What decision it helps make: Helps me to decide whether to carry an umbrella or plan outdoor activities.

Online Shopping History
Who collects this data: Amazon or Flipkart
What decision it helps make: It helps to recommend products and helps me track my spending habits.

One Question I Want to Answer Using Data:
“How can I use my daily routine data (sleep, study time, and phone usage) to become more productive?"
